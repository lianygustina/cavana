L O C A T I O N

Strategically located at Jalan Kenanga, 500 meters from Fedex Building, South of Jakarta. Close to various national and international school, reputable hospitals, supermarkets, and eateries.Easy access, 5 minutes to outer ring road highway and Pondok Indah Mall.

M A S T E R P L A N

CAVANA Kenanga offers extra generous space in its layout with excellent quality standard. Located in one of the most prominent area in South Jakarta, Jalan Kenanga, Kebayoran Lama. A luxurious and serene neighbourhood with various access to main road TB Simatupang and Outier Ring Road Toll
